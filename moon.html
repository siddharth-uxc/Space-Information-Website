🌙 The Moon

About The Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and the fifth-largest moon in our Solar System. It is the brightest and largest object in our night sky.

🌟 Key Facts

  • Type: Natural satellite
  • Distance from Earth: 384,400 km
  • Diameter: 3,474 km (27% of Earth)
  • Orbital Period: 27.3 days
  • Age: 4.5 billion years
  • Surface Gravity: 1.62 m/s² (16.6% of Earth)
  • Temperature: -173°C to 127°C

💡 Interesting Facts

  • 12 humans have walked on the Moon (1969-1972)
  • Moon's gravity creates ocean tides on Earth
  • Always shows same face to Earth (tidally locked)
  • Moving away from Earth at 3.8 cm per year
  • Footprints will last millions of years (no atmosphere)
  • Formed from a giant impact with Earth 4.5 billion years ago
  • Has water ice in permanently shadowed craters
