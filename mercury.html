☿️ Mercury

About Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our Solar System and the closest to the Sun. It's a rocky planet with a heavily cratered surface similar to our Moon.

🌟 Key Facts

  • Type: Terrestrial (rocky) planet
  • Distance from Sun: 57.9 million km
  • Diameter: 4,879 km (38% of Earth)
  • Orbital Period: 88 Earth days
  • Day Length: 59 Earth days
  • Surface Temperature: -173°C to 427°C
  • Moons: 0

💡 Interesting Facts

  • Mercury has the most extreme temperature variations
  • Named after the Roman messenger god
  • One day on Mercury is longer than one year!
  • Has a large iron core (75% of planet diameter)
  • Surface is covered with impact craters
  • No atmosphere to protect from meteorites
  • Caloris Basin crater is 1,550 km wide
