🪐 Saturn

About Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in our Solar System. It is best known for its stunning ring system made of billions of ice and rock particles.

🌟 Key Facts

  • Type: Gas giant
  • Distance from Sun: 1.43 billion km
  • Diameter: 116,460 km (9.4× Earth)
  • Orbital Period: 29.46 Earth years
  • Day Length: 10.7 hours
  • Moons: 146 confirmed (most in Solar System)
  • Density: Less than water — it would float!

💡 Interesting Facts

  • Rings are only 10-100 meters thick but 280,000 km wide
  • Titan has a thick atmosphere and liquid methane lakes
  • Enceladus shoots water geysers from subsurface ocean
  • Hexagonal storm at north pole is wider than Earth
  • Cassini studied Saturn for 13 years
  • Winds can reach 1,800 km/h
  • Rings are 99.9% pure water ice
