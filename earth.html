🌍 Earth

About Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. About 71% of Earth's surface is covered with water, earning it the nickname "Blue Planet".

🌟 Key Facts

  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Age: 4.54 billion years
  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Distance from Sun: 149.6 million km (1 AU)
  • Orbital Period: 365.25 days
  • Day Length: 24 hours
  • Moons: 1 (The Moon)

💡 Interesting Facts

  • Only planet not named after a Roman or Greek god
  • Home to over 8.7 million species of life
  • Atmosphere: 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen
  • 70% of Earth's fresh water is frozen in ice caps
  • Magnetic field protects us from solar radiation
  • Highest point: Mt. Everest (8,849m)
  • Deepest point: Mariana Trench (10,994m)
  • The only known planet with plate tectonics
