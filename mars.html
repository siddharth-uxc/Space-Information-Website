🔴 Mars

About Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System. Known as the "Red Planet" due to iron oxide on its surface giving it a reddish appearance.

🌟 Key Facts

  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Distance from Sun: 227.9 million km
  • Diameter: 6,779 km (53% of Earth)
  • Orbital Period: 687 Earth days
  • Day Length: 24 hours 37 minutes
  • Surface Temperature: -87°C to -5°C
  • Moons: 2 (Phobos and Deimos)

💡 Interesting Facts

  • Has the largest volcano: Olympus Mons (21.9 km high)
  • Valles Marineris canyon is 4,000 km long
  • Has the largest dust storms in the Solar System
  • Evidence of ancient river valleys and lake beds
  • Perseverance rover is searching for signs of ancient life
  • NASA plans human missions in the 2030s
  • Gravity is 38% of Earth's
