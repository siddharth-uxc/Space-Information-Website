🔵 Uranus

About Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and the third-largest in our Solar System. It's an ice giant with a unique sideways rotation, making it one of the most unusual planets.

🌟 Key Facts

  • Type: Ice giant
  • Distance from Sun: 2.87 billion km
  • Diameter: 50,724 km (4× Earth)
  • Orbital Period: 84 Earth years
  • Day Length: 17.24 hours
  • Axial Tilt: 98° (rotates on its side!)
  • Moons: 27 known

💡 Interesting Facts

  • Only planet that rotates on its side
  • Each pole gets 42 years of sunlight then 42 years of darkness
  • Discovered by William Herschel in 1781
  • Has 13 faint rings discovered in 1977
  • Moons named after Shakespeare characters
  • Coldest planetary atmosphere (-224°C)
  • Blue-green color from methane absorbing red light
