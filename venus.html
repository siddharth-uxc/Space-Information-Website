♀️ Venus

About Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and Earth's closest planetary neighbor. Often called Earth's "twin" due to similar size, but has extreme conditions.

🌟 Key Facts

  • Type: Terrestrial planet
  • Distance from Sun: 108.2 million km
  • Diameter: 12,104 km (95% of Earth)
  • Orbital Period: 225 Earth days
  • Day Length: 243 Earth days (rotates backwards!)
  • Surface Temperature: 462°C (hottest planet)
  • Moons: 0

💡 Interesting Facts

  • Hottest planet despite being second from the Sun
  • Rotates in opposite direction to most planets
  • Thick atmosphere is 96.5% carbon dioxide
  • Atmospheric pressure is 90 times Earth's
  • Brightest natural object in night sky after Moon
  • Named after Roman goddess of love
  • Has over 1,600 volcanoes
