🔷 Neptune

About Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and most distant planet from the Sun. This ice giant was the first planet found through mathematical predictions rather than direct observation.

🌟 Key Facts

  • Type: Ice giant
  • Distance from Sun: 4.50 billion km
  • Diameter: 49,528 km (3.9× Earth)
  • Orbital Period: 164.8 Earth years
  • Day Length: 16.1 hours
  • Moons: 16 known
  • Wind Speed: Up to 2,100 km/h

💡 Interesting Facts

  • Discovered through math before being observed in 1846
  • Has the strongest winds in the Solar System
  • Triton orbits backwards — likely a captured Kuiper Belt object
  • Triton has nitrogen geysers
  • Radiates 2.6× more energy than it receives from Sun
  • Has 6 faint rings named after astronomers
  • Deep blue color from methane in the atmosphere
